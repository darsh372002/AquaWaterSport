Faq’s

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age requirement?

Bring a towel, sunscreen, a bottle of water, and a change of clothes. We recommend wearing a swimsuit or boardshorts under your wetsuit. We provide the surfboard and wetsuit unless otherwise specified.

Are the instructors for Surfing certified?

Yes, all our instructors are certified professionals with training in surf instruction, ocean safety, and first aid. Your safety and learning experience are our top priorities.

Do I need to know how to Surfing?

Not at all! Our lessons are designed for all levels — from complete beginners to those looking to improve their skills. We’ll guide you step by step at a pace that suits you.

How Much it take to complete a course?

Wear a swimsuit or boardshorts that you’re comfortable in. We’ll provide a wetsuit or rash guard if needed. Don’t forget flip-flops or sandals and sunscreen for before and after your session.

What is the cost of the Surfing course?

The cost of our surfing courses varies based on duration and package type. Introductory sessions typically start at ₹1,999, with multi-day courses available at discounted rates. For the latest pricing and offers, please visit our official website or contact us directly.

Any medical requirements for Surfing?

While surfing is suitable for most healthy individuals, basic fitness and swimming ability are recommended. If you have any medical conditions like asthma, heart issues, or recent injuries, please consult your doctor and inform our team before booking. Your safety is our top priority.

What equipment I need to the Surfing?

You don’t need to bring any surf gear. Aqua provides everything you need, including a surfboard, leash, wetsuit or rash vest, and safety gear. Just bring your enthusiasm and we’ll handle the rest!

What should I wear to the Surfing course?

We recommend wearing a swimsuit or boardshorts under the wetsuit or rash guard we provide. Also, bring a towel, sunscreen, flip-flops, and a change of clothes for after the session.
